Module 04: Neural Architectures

L4.2: Understanding Transformer Attention

In this lesson, we explore the core mechanism that revolutionized NLP: the Attention mechanism. Unlike traditional RNNs that process sequences word-by-word, Transformers allow the model to focus on different parts of the input sequence simultaneously.

"Attention is all you need" — the paper that defined the modern AI era. We will dissect the Scaled Dot-Product Attention formula and its implications for long-range dependencies.

Key concepts we will cover include Query, Key, and Value vectors, the Softmax activation over scores, and why scaling by the square root of the dimension is critical for gradient stability.
